Bridging Research and Patient Care

Dr. Anupam Das is a rare clinician-scientist operating at the vanguard of modern dermatology. Through an unwavering commitment to both laboratory discovery and bedside empathy, he solves the most challenging dermatological cases in Eastern India.

His core clinical work focuses extensively on dermatophytosis, allergic disorders, metabolic syndrome, melasma, and arsenicosis management. As a Gold Medalist from WBUHS, his treatment protocols are defined by precision rather than presumption.
